About Us

PDP Youths Mobilization Gombi LG (PYM) is a youth-driven platform under the People's Democratic Party (PDP), committed to empowering young people through unity, innovation, and active participation.

We believe that together, United We Rise, United We Thrive. Our organization serves as a bridge between the youth and the political process, ensuring that young voices are heard and valued in decision-making.

Through various initiatives, we aim to create an enabling environment where young people can develop their potential, contribute to community development, and become leaders of tomorrow.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

  • To mobilize and empower youths for political, social, and economic development.
  • To inspire leadership, inclusivity, and innovation in our community.
  • To strengthen the role of PDP in building a united and thriving Nigeria.

Our Vision

To build a generation of committed, united, and empowered youths who will drive positive transformation in Gombi LG and beyond.
