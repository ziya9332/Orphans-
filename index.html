PDP Youths Mobilization – Gombi Local Government

“United We Rise, United We Thrive”

Aims and Objectives

  • To mobilize the youths of Gombi Local Government towards active political participation.
  • To promote unity, peace, and progress within PDP youths.
  • To ensure inclusion and empowerment of young people in political and community development.
  • To uphold transparency, accountability, and discipline in leadership.
  • To support the vision and mission of the People’s Democratic Party (PDP).
  • To organize developmental programs, sensitizations, and grassroots campaigns.
